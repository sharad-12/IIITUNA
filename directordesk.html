Director's Desk

Prof. S. Selvakumar

Our Institute was established in the year 2014-15 as an Institute of National Importance under the MoE, GoI, by an IIIT (PPP) Act of Parliament No. 23 of 2017. I assumed charge as the Founder Director of this Institute on 05, July 2018. Prior to joining, I was invited to attend the Governing Body Meeting of the Institute in the Office of Additional Secretary, MoE, as the Director designate and special invitee in which Master Plan of the Institute was scheduled to be approved. As the Master Plan was prepared without the user requirement specification from the Institute, the approval of the Master Plan was deferred. Then the architects prepared the Master Plan based on the user requirement specification given by the Institute and got approved.
