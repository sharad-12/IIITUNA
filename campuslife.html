Life on Campus

Culture is the widening of the mind and of the spirit.

Student Culture

It’s a good place to meet like- minded people and learn and grow together. The campus community forms a close knit group and you make friends for life here. The campus, the people and this life has made a stronger and confident person.

Clubs

Interested in something? There will be a club for it. If there isn't one, you can open your own. (assuming, of course, that you get enough people interested in the same activity) More importantly, you don't have to approach a professor for opening a club, you just put up a proposal in front of the "Students' Gymkhana"(composed almost entirely of students in executive positions). If the Gymkhana approves it, you are done.

Cultural Council

“A nation's culture resides in the hearts and in the soul of its people.”- Mahatma Gandhi

The Cultural Council of the Students' Gymkhana organizes a plethora of activities spanning over the whole year. With a calendar punctuated by scores of workshops and classes over the year, including the summers, the Council provides the students profuse platforms to pursue various cultural genres, train themselves, perform on stage or just appreciate various cultural arts. The students are also given a chance to hone their skills and compete with other prolific colleges in intercollegiate cultural festivals. The council gives ample opportunities to the students to cement them into the cultural scene at IIIT UNA through events like MRIDANG, YALGAR, MERAKI and other club events.

Eunoia

To inculate the habit of literature reading and its various forms. To showcase and promote various cultural activities. Promoting the concept of flow & grow by providing a platform for students to enunciate their works,ideas, and beliefs. Debates on current national issues.

Force

Force: Forum for Computer Engineers Computer science society of IIIT Una Focuses on open source software technologies Bulding coding culture among student Encouraging students to participate in event such as Google summer of code(Gsoc) Organising hackathons on latest problems Workshops on web/app development and UI/U Conducting Seminars and webinars by industry experts

Epmoc

Works for creating a collaborative and inclusive environment amongst students Aims to organize events from different domains to encourage students to showcase their skills Bridging the gap between seniors and college alumni by social networks Conducting online workshops to improve debate, communication and interpersonal skills

Aavesh

Aavesh: The Electronics society Strives to help the students acquire new skills in the field of Electronics Exposure of latest tools and technologies to the members Conducting workshops and organising lectures from industry and academics Workshop on MATLAB,Simulink and Arduino

Pixcel

Celebrating vibrance in multitude of eye pleasing sights and breathtaking instances, Photography club aims to capture them all. It strives to provide opportunities to the students to hone their creative skills and create the photographs that speak their own word. Photography club has high grade equipment in terms of digital SLRs and lenses. It has a studio with all professional level setup.

Games and Sports Council

IIIT UNA is not just about academics but also has world class sports facilities. It has one cricket fields, a 400 metres track, separate floodlit football and hockey fields, floodlit basketball courts , volleyball and tennis courts, indoor basketball and badminton stadiums, indoor arenas for weightlifting and squash, a skating rink, a taekwondo arena, table tennis rooms, a yoga room, two well­equipped gymnasiums and the list goes on and on.
