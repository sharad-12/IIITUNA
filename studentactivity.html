Students Activity

Prog-o-thon

In conjunction with the Technical Fest 2022, the Indian Institute of Information Technology Una will host the first-ever biggest hackathon, ‘Prog-a-thon’. Prog-a-thon is a hackathon that aims to solve institute-specific problems such as problem statements for attendance systems and other real-world issues. It is the intent of this hackathon to solve real-world problems as well as to help students gain teamwork experience, knowledge, and motivation to solve problems in their technical careers ahead.

Technical Workshop

A technical workshop is an interactive learning session designed to provide hands-on experience and in-depth knowledge about a specific technology, tool, or skill. Typically led by experts, workshops offer practical insights, problem-solving exercises to help participants grasp complex concepts. Attendees actively engage with the subject matter through discussion, activities, tasks, fostering a understand. Workshops are often used for skill development, collaboration, and enhancing expertise in various fields, ranging from software development and engineering to arts and crafts.

Robo Race

RoboRace is a cutting-edge motorsport competition where autonomous vehicles, driven by advanced AI systems, compete on racetracks. The race aims to push the boundaries of AI autonomous vehicle, fostering innovation and collaboration among engineers, and researchers. These high-speed challenges showcase the capabilities of self-driving technology and its potential for the future of transportation. RoboRace serves as a platform to accelerate the development of safe and efficient autonomous systems, showcasing their abilities in real-world scenarios and paving the way for advancements in the automotive industry.

Yalgaar Sports Fest

A college sports fest is an annual event that brings together students from different colleges to compete in a wide range of sports and athletic activities. It's a celebration sportsmanship, healthy competition. The fest typically includes various sports football, basketball, volleyball, athletics, and more. Alongside the games, there are cultural events, entertainment shows, and social gatherings that create a vibrant and energetic atmosphere. College sports fests promote physical fitness, camaraderie, and a sense of belonging among students while showcasing their talents on a larger platform.
